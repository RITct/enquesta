Enquesta 2016

A national level intercollegiate quiz competition organized by Govt.Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology, kottayam Enquesta 2016 is a national level intercollegate quiz competition organized by Govt.Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology and co-ordinated by Department of Computer Science and Engineering as the part of Silver Jubilee celebrations of Rajiv Gandhi institute of technology.
